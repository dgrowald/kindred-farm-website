Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Kindred Farm ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with us, including through our website and messaging platforms.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, or other contact details you provide to us.
  • Communications: The content of messages you send to us, including through email and messaging platforms such as WhatsApp.
  • Technical Information: Basic information about your visit to our website, such as your IP address and browser type, collected automatically through standard web server logs.

Messaging Platforms

We may communicate with you through messaging platforms including WhatsApp. When you contact us or opt in to receive messages through these platforms, we may collect:

  • Your phone number
  • Your name or profile name as provided by the platform
  • The content of messages you send to us
  • Message timestamps and delivery status

We use this information solely to communicate with you about Kindred Farm, respond to your inquiries, and provide information you've requested.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your questions and communications
  • Send you information about Kindred Farm that you've requested
  • Maintain records of our communications
  • Improve how we communicate with our community

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When necessary to provide services you've requested (for example, using WhatsApp's platform to send you messages)
  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Request to stop receiving messages from us at any time
  • Ask us what personal information we have about you
  • Request that we delete your personal information
  • Update or correct your contact information

To exercise any of these choices, please contact us at the email address below.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated revision date.
