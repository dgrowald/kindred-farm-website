About Kindred Farm

Kindred Farm is a small-scale land stewardship and rewilding initiative. Our focus is on soil health, biodiversity, ecological restoration, and relationship-based agriculture.

We're putting down roots. More to come soon.

What We Do

We work with the land rather than against it. Our approach centers on:

  • Building and maintaining healthy soil through natural methods
  • Supporting native plant and animal species
  • Restoring ecological balance to the land we steward
  • Fostering meaningful connections between people and place

Our Values

We believe in patience, observation, and working at the pace of natural systems. Good land stewardship takes time. We're here for the long term.
